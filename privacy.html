Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-17

This Privacy Policy explains how BPTracker handles your information.

1. Data Collection

BPTracker is designed to store blood pressure records on your device. We do not require account registration for core usage.

2. Data Usage

  • Your records are used to display trends, statistics, and export reports inside the app.
  • Data is not sold to third parties.

3. Data Storage

  • Health records are stored locally on your device by default.
  • If you choose export features, exported files are created based on your action.

4. Permissions

BPTracker may request notification permission for reminder features. You can disable permissions anytime in system settings.
